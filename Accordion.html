• Start with compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, and bench press.
  • Focus on progressive overload to gradually increase weight and reps.
  • Ensure proper form to avoid injury and maximize muscle growth.

  • Try high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to burn fat effectively.
  • Long-duration steady-state cardio like running or cycling can also be great.
  • Remember to combine cardio with strength training for optimal results.

  • Ensure a balanced diet with adequate protein, healthy fats, and carbohydrates.
  • Stay hydrated before, during, and after your workouts.
  • Eat a post-workout snack with protein to help with muscle recovery.
