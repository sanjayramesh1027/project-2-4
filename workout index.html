Workout Tips

  • Start with compound exercises (squats, deadlifts, bench press).
  • Gradually increase weight to avoid injury.
  • Ensure proper form to target muscles effectively.

  • Incorporate both steady-state and interval training.
  • Track your heart rate to optimize fat burning.
  • Start with 20-30 minutes of cardio and build from there.

  • Eat a balanced diet with proteins, carbs, and healthy fats.
  • Stay hydrated before, during, and after workouts.
  • Have a post-workout snack that includes protein to help muscle recovery.
